Delivery and Return Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2023

Thank you for choosing to shop at MySafePrice.com ("Website"). We aim to provide you with a seamless shopping experience and exceptional customer service. Please read the following Delivery and Return Policy to understand our practices regarding product delivery and returns.

1. Delivery
1.1 Delivery Areas:
We offer delivery services within Kathmandu Valley. For areas outside Kathmandu Valley, please refer to our Shipping Information page for details on availability and applicable charges.

1.2 Delivery Time:
We strive to process and deliver orders promptly. Delivery timeframes may vary depending on product availability, order processing, and location. Estimated delivery time is same day or next day within kathmandu valley and 1-3 days outside kathmandu valley. Please note that these are approximate estimates and actual delivery times may vary.

1.3 Cash on Delivery (COD):
We understand the convenience of Cash on Delivery (COD) for our customers within Kathmandu Valley. You can choose the COD option during the checkout process. Please ensure that you have the exact cash amount ready at the time of delivery.

2. Returns and Refunds
2.1 Defective or Damaged Products:
We take utmost care to ensure that the products you receive are of the highest quality. In the rare event that you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact our Customer Support within 48 hours of receiving the item. We will arrange for the return of the product and offer you a replacement or a refund as per our discretion.

2.2 Return Eligibility:
To be eligible for a return, the product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as when you received it. Any accompanying tags, labels, or accessories should also be included.

2.3 Return Process:
If you wish to return a product for any reason other than a defect or damage, please contact our Customer Support within 48 hours of receiving the item. We will provide you with instructions on how to return the product. Return shipping costs will be borne by the customer, unless otherwise specified by us.

2.4 Refunds:
Once your returned product is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed using the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that the time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

3. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our Delivery and Return Policy, please contact our Customer Support at 9819060344/9800909096. Our dedicated team is ready to assist you and provide further clarification as needed.

By using our Website and placing an order, you acknowledge and agree to comply with our Delivery and Return Policy.
